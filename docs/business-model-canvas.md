# Business Model Canvas — Nazarato

Generated by /office-hours on 2026-05-31
Companion to `docs/pivot.md`. Status: DRAFT v1 (two key decisions locked).

> Trust layer for Iranian shops. Lead with the Instagram scam-lookup wedge;
> ratings live in the schema and turn on later. Consumer side free; shops pay
> for a verified badge later. See `docs/pivot.md` for the full reasoning and the
> survey evidence behind these blocks.

---

## 1. Customer Segments

- **Buyers (free side — the engine):** Iranian women 20–40, shop Instagram weekly
  (clothing, beauty, home, jewelry), live in Telegram, forward scam warnings.
  Burned, or one DM from someone who was.
- **Venters (seed-contributor sub-segment):** the ~33% who already post complaints
  publicly (own page, comments, police). Your first review/report writers.
- **Shop owners (paying side — later):** honest IG/online shops that look identical
  to scammers and lose sales for it.

## 2. Value Propositions

- **Buyers (launch):** "Before you pay, know if this shop is a scam." Any handle,
  reports + evidence, 10 seconds, no login.
- **Buyers (later):** "Will this shop disappoint me?" — shipping speed, photo
  accuracy, support, from real reviews.
- **Shop owners:** "Prove you're not a scammer." Verified badge + claimed page that
  converts hesitant buyers.

## 3. Channels

- Shareable `/ig/{handle}` URL — the core viral loop (Telegram / X / DMs)
- Persian SEO: one indexable page per handle (`بررسی [handle]`, `[handle] کلاهبردار`)
- Telegram bot `@nazarato_bot` (week 4+, distribution lever)
- PWA install for repeat users
- Own Instagram page (optional — reposting scam alerts)

## 4. Customer Relationships

- Self-serve + automated: no-login lookup, 30-second report form (screenshot required)
- Community-reported, founder-moderated (trust depends on resisting gaming)
- "Shop can respond" flow — fairness + legal cover

## 5. Revenue Streams — **LOCKED: shop subscription for verified badge**

- **Primary:** honest shops pay a monthly fee for a **verified badge + claimed/managed
  page**. Starts month 2+.
- **Consumer side: free forever.**
- **In reserve (not now):** analytics/response tools, "best of category." Avoid
  paid placement — it reads as pay-to-rank and erodes trust.
- ⚠️ **Trust guardrail (non-negotiable):** the badge verifies **identity/ownership,
  not quality**. A paying shop with bad reviews still shows bad reviews. Reports and
  ratings are independent of payment, always. This line is what separates Nazarato
  from a pay-to-play review site. Bake it into copy + policy from day one.
- Open: Iranian payment rail (no Stripe) must be resolved before charging — see Partnerships.

## 6. Key Resources

- **The reputation index** — the moat; compounds with every lookup and report.
- Existing codebase (Next 16 + React 19 + Supabase) — a parts bin to re-aim, not extend.
- `nazarato.com` domain + Persian SEO position.
- The founder — solo, Persian-native, the moderator and seed-er.

## 7. Key Activities

- **Seeding the index** (manual, pre-launch + ongoing) — the make-or-break activity.
- Moderation + defense against bad-faith reports.
- Persian SEO + tuning the share loop.
- **Re-aiming the build to the wedge** (and *not* shipping new platform features).

## 8. Key Partnerships — **LOCKED: ingest + credit, no formal deal**

- **Telegram scam-warning channels (seed source):** pull public reports, document
  with evidence, **credit the source channel**. No negotiation. Watch: channels may
  perceive you as competing — keep credit visible and consider partnering later if a
  channel objects or wants in.
- **Iranian payment rail (Zarinpal / IDPay / etc.):** required before shop billing.
  Resolve in month 2, not now.
- **eNamad / official trust bodies:** ignore for launch; optionally surface their
  seal as one signal among many later.

## 9. Cost Structure

- Hosting: Vercel + Supabase (low at launch).
- Gemini (voice) + Kavenegar (SMS OTP) — usage-based, small.
- One-time legal review of the public "Reported" badge before launch.
- **Founder time — the real cost.** Every hour on a non-wedge feature is the
  expensive line item.

## Riskiest assumptions to test first (cheapest → confirm before scaling)

1. **People will look up handles.** (500+ organic lookups in week 4 — the core demand test.)
2. **People will share the card URLs.** (5+ unsolicited shares — the growth engine.)
3. **The 56%-won't-contribute landmine is survivable** because lookups + seeded data
   carry the value without relying on user reports. (Watch report rate, but don't bet on it.)
4. **Shops will pay for a verified badge** once consumer traffic gives it signaling value. (Test last, month 2+.)
